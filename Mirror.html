MIRROR MAGAZINE

(editorial design, typography)

  • → 2024-2025
  • → Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign
  • → 8.5" x 11"
  • Published by Modern and Classical Languages Department
  • Editors: Claudia Liu, Roan McAuley, Lilly Hessari, Peregrin Hunjun
  • Art Direction & Design: Sofia Choi

Mirror Magazine is a publication that highlights the activities of the Modern and Classical Languages Department at Western Washington University. It is written in both English and Mandarin, taking submissions from both the campus community and international contributors. It aims to promote Chinese culture and language abroad, fostering cross-cultural connections between students.
