ROME GROCERY

(branding, typography, motion)

  • → Illustrator, After Effects
  • Branding, motion graphics, and handmade type

The goal of this project was to refresh the visual identity of a real local business. Rome grocery has been in Bellingham, WA since 1925. I aimed to create a fun, modern brand with a strong visual impact to play on the emergence of trendy food & drink brands.
