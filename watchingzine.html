WATCHING YOU WATCHING ME

(editorial design, typography)

  • → Fall 2024
  • → Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign
  • → 5.5" x 8"
  • Self Published
  • Original Articles: Who's Watching by Maria Manoukian, Aesthetic Consumerism and the Violence of Photography by Maria Popova, The Subversive Act of Photographing Palestinian Life by Adam Rouhana

Watching You Watching Me is a zine comprised of three articles that touch on themes of surveillance, privacy, and the politics of cameras. Our lives are inundated with screens and imagesâ€”I am curious about the ways this immersion influences our behavior towards other people, environments, and ourselves.
