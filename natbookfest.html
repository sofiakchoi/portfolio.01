NATIONAL BOOK FESTIVAL

(branding, typography, UI)

  • → Winter 2025
  • → Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma
  • DSGN 340 Graphic Design II
  • Branding, typographic design, website UI

Rebranding project for the Library of Congress 2026 National Book Festival. As the largest literary event in the country, I felt the branding should be appealing to a modern audience of readers by nodding to the aesthetic trends of young literati. I aimed to celebrate book design by creating a branding system where typography was the star. The designs use microtype to evoke printed matter.
